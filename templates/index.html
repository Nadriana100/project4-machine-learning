PREDICTING HOUSE PRICES



Considerations to determine a house price

1. Zone: location, road access, alley access, proximity to other buildings/activities
2. Size: square feet, number of floors, number of rooms and bathrooms, presence of garage, pool, fireplace, porch areas
3. Quality: material quality, finish of the house/roof/pool
4. Utilities: heating, air conditioning
5. Timeline: year built/remodeled, year sold, type of sale
6. Price
